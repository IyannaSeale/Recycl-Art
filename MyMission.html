Mission Statement


This is my mission Statement:

“To Minimize Municipal Solid Waste and Land Pollution
By Turning Trash Into Art”





The secondary objectives of this project are as follows:

  • To create employment in the community: Jobs such as Cleaners, Cutters, Supervisors etc. will be created
  • To increase environmental consciousness: This initiative will teach children, teens and adults in the community how to be more environmentally friendly
  • To bring Saint Lucian communities closer together: Taking part in these activities together will build community spirit and bring the community as a whole closer together
