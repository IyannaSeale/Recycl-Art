How Does It Work?

As previously mentioned, this project will turn municipal solid waste into art. People of all ages will fill out the form in the "Get Started" section of this website. After the form is reviewed, we will send special Recycl-Art bags that they are to place the waste from the project into. The waste being put into the bags can come from either their homes, workplaces or can be picked up from a littered area. Food and other biodegradables are not to be placed in these bags. The bags will also be recycled and used in the art to be made.


  

The person will then call or email Recycl-Art and our workers will come via truck to pick the bags up and transport them to Recycl-Art's building. For every piece of trash sent in, the sender will receive $0.20, which they will be able to collect at the Recycl-Art building.


When the bags get to the building, they will be taken to the processing department where their contents will be cleaned, disinfected and sorted based on colour. The creative design team will come up with art piece ideas based on the trash received and these ideas will be posted to the website's "Projects" page.




Senders will receive emails and notifications from the site to alert them about when the trash can be put together. On a specific day, senders will come to the Recycl-Art building to work together as teams on putting together the trash into the design that the team came up with.
